I am an associate professor in the department of economics at the University of Colorado, Boulder, and a research associate at the National Bureau of Economic Research. My research focuses on regional development in the United States. I am also interested in the effects of regulation and industrial policy.

I am visiting the Smith Institute for Political Economy and Philosophy at Chapman University during academic year 2023-24. I will teach an undergraduate course on the economic history of the United States and co-organize (with Jared Rubin) the Chapman Economic History Seminar.
