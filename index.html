Taylor Jaworski

Associate Professor of Economics and Interim Director of the Benson Center
University of Colorado Boulder
Research Associate, National Bureau of Economic Research

I am an economic historian. My research focuses on the United States in the nineteenth and twentieth centuries. In particular, I am interested in how economic geography shapes regional development, the influence of regulation and industrial policy on market structure, and the role of human capital in labor markets.
